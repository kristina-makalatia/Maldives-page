THE TOP 5 LUXURY RESORTS IN THE MALDIVES

1. One&Only Reethi Rah

The One & Only Reethi Rah is in Maldives’ North Male Atoll. This luxurious tropical getaway overlooks the Indian Ocean and features an infinity pool, free Wi-Fi and a peaceful private beach. The elegant villas at One & Only Reethi Rah are situated either over the lagoon or by the beach and have beautiful ocean views. Flat-screen cable/satellite TVs, iPod docks and DVD players can be enjoyed in all rooms. Guests may enjoy massage treatments at One&Only Spa or rejuvenate with a game of tennis at the outdoor courts. The resort also has a beach club -the One Tribe, which caters to teenagers and KidsOnly, a spacious activity place for children facilitated by well-trained staff.
Built over the water, the Tapasake Restaurant serves modern Japanese cuisine, and the beachside Fanditha Restaurant has Middle Eastern food and champagne. Reethi serves Mediterranean cuisine. Other dining options include Rah Bar and 24-hour in-villa dining. The One & Only Reethi Rah is a 1-hour and 15-minute luxury yacht ride or a 45-minute speedboat ride from Male International Airport. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.5 for a two-person trip.
Price per night about: 1960$

The resort has a playground and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant.A flat-screen TV, as well as an iPad are provided. Certain rooms include a sitting area where you can relax. You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom equipped with a bathtub. Extras include bathrobes and slippers. You will find free shuttle service, a 24-hour front desk, hairdresser's and gift shop at the property.
This resort has a private beach area and free use of bicycles and bike rental are available. A number of activities are available in the area, such as snorkeling and windsurfing. The nearest airport is Male Airport, 35.4 km from OZEN by Atmosphere at Maadhoo. This property also has one of the top-rated locations in South Male Atoll! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.6 for a two-person trip.
